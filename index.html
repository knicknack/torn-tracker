Data management

About

Foreign stock data is crowd-sourced from YATA (the same source TornPDA, TornTools, and torntravel use). YATA's rate limit is 10 calls per hour per IP — this tool caches data for 5 minutes. Restock predictions use the community heuristic (sell-out ÷ 2, rounded to 15-min tick). All your personal data stays in this browser.
